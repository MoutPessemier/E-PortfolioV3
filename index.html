About me

Aspiring young photographer

Ever since I was little, I've had an interest in photography. My dad gave me throwaway cameras to take with me on school trips. Later on, he got me further hooked by buying a DSLR camera. I started to use it more and more, do more research into the basics of photography and ever since I've loved taking pictures, playing around with the settings to create special effect, etc.

A few years ago, I bought my own camera, a Canon EOS RP. In the summer of 2022, I dicided to take the leap and start up my own photography business, Mout Pessemier Studios (MPS).

Since the start of MPS, I've done a few photoshoots from which you can find 9 images per project. I'm also planning on doing videography and graphical design.
